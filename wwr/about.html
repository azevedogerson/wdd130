History

Founded in 2025 by Gerson Azevedo in the vibrant city of Beira, Mozambique, our conservatory began with a bold
vision: to redefine musical education for a new generation. Although we are a young institution, our passion for
excellence is timeless. We offer an inspiring environment where musicians of all levels can master the art of the
piano, guided by experts dedicated to turning raw talent into contemporary mastery. We believe that the future of
music starts right here in Beira, finding your own voice through the strings.
