Mozambique

Mozambique is located on the southeastern coast of Africa, bordered to
the east by the Indian Ocean, and shares borders with Tanzania (North),
Malawi and Zambia (Northwest), Zimbabwe (West), South Africa and
Eswatini (Southwest), and is separated from Madagascar by the Mozambique Channel.
